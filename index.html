About Dem Pigeons

Pigeonhole LLC fatefully began with a group of friends sitting around their favorite pub (or is it café?) tossing back pints and wines - you can probably guess who drinks which. The discussion was on the influx of film projects into West Virginia, more specifically the Eastern Panhandle, our home. We all are very passionate about WV and love that these productions are taking place here.

Seeing the positive impact these productions have on the local economy, we wanted to get involved in supporting this mutually beneficial situation. Given our team’s unique, but perfectly dove-tailed (pigeon-tailed?) set of skills, our initial focus was on helping production companies successfully take advantage of the lucrative incentives provided by the state. Pigeonhole LLC will do everything to ensure your production gets the assistance and tailored services required to make the process of receiving your WV tax credits and incentives as seamless as possible.

We believe in self starting entrepreneurial ventures, and that’s why we have started this project from the ground up with our own boot leather. No outside investors, no second mortgages, no credit card debt so we only answer to our customers.

Among other offerings, we’re in the process of developing software which will also be of great use to the entertainment industry. But don’t ask us to let that cat out of the bag just yet! We’ll show you that when the time is right...
